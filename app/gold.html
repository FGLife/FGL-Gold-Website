Key Product Features:

Synergy Global Advantage Gold® is a flexible premium indexed universal life insurance product that offers competitive interest crediting options with added “living benefits” to help your client and their family financially in the event of a critical or terminal illness.

  • Simple product design; Easy to understand and explain
  • 14% cap rate on 100% participation S&P 500® annual point-to-point index interest crediting option
  • 1.25% spread rate on uncapped Dow Jones Index interest crediting option
  • 15% cap on Gold Commodity index interest crediting option
  • Non-medical underwriting option available for qualifying clients
  • Guaranteed maximum variable loan rate of 5.5%*
  • 0.25% persistency bonus beginning in the 11th policy year**
  • 15 year death benefit guarantee with minimum premiums
  • Living benefit riders for critical illness and terminal illness as well as overloan protection rider***
  • Surrender charge waiver on excess premium+
